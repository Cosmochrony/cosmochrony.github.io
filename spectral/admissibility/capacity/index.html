Spectral Capacity

Global aggregation of admissible spectral amplitudes.

Programme overview

Overview

Spectral capacity aggregates the admissible amplitudes of all representation sectors of a relational Cayley graph. It provides a global measure of admissible relational capacity.

Capacity functional

The spectral capacity is defined as

$C(G,S)=\sum_{\rho,\lambda_\rho>0}\frac{(\dim\rho)^2}{\sqrt{\lambda_\rho}}$

where the sum runs over the irreducible representations of the group associated with the Cayley graph.
