Spectral Gram Rigidity

Structural selection among finite SU(2) subgroups.

Programme overview

Overview

This article studies the Gram geometry of neutral generating sets in SU(2). It shows that the structure of the Gram matrix rigidly constrains the group generated by the corresponding traceless elements.

Main result

The Gram matrix

$G_{st}=u_s\cdot u_t$

combined with the isotropy of the second-moment tensor determines the admissible group structures.

  • anisotropic second moment → dihedral groups
  • orthogonal Gram structure → quaternion group Q₈
  • non-trivial Gram angles → binary polyhedral groups
